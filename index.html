Project Title: Bank Loan Analysis Power BI Project

Project Overview:

This project involved analyzing bank loan data to identify key factors influencing loan approvals, understand customer demographics, and improve the decision-making process for loan applications. The goal was to provide actionable insights to the bankâ€™s loan approval team and help them make data-driven decisions.

Objectives:

  • Identify patterns and trends in loan approval and rejection.
  • Analyze customer demographics and their impact on loan status.
  • Visualize key metrics that help in understanding loan performance.

Data Used:

  • Customer demographics (age, gender, income, education, etc.).
  • Loan application details (loan amount, loan type, tenure, etc.).
  • Loan status (approved, rejected, pending).
  • Historical loan performance data.

Tools and Techniques:

  • Power BI: Used for data visualization and dashboard creation.
  • DAX: Utilized to create calculated columns and measures for deeper insights.
  • Data Cleaning and Transformation: Performed using Power Query in Power BI.
  • Key Visualizations: Used bar charts, pie charts, KPIs, and slicers to present data intuitively.

Key Insights:

  • Identified that loan approval rates were highest among customers with a steady income and good credit history.
  • Observed that younger applicants had lower approval rates due to limited credit history.
  • Noted that the majority of rejected loans were due to high debt-to-income ratios.
  • Highlighted geographical trends showing certain regions with higher default rates.

Challenges Faced:

Handling missing or inconsistent data in customer demographics, ensuring data accuracy in the transformation process, and designing the dashboard to make it user-friendly for stakeholders with varying levels of technical expertise.

Results:

The analysis provided the bank with a comprehensive view of the factors impacting loan approvals and rejections. Enabled the loan approval team to refine their criteria, reducing the risk of defaults, and improved decision-making efficiency by providing a centralized dashboard for loan performance tracking.

Conclusion:

This project demonstrated how data visualization and analysis can drive better decision-making in the banking sector. By leveraging Power BI, complex data was transformed into actionable insights, helping the bank optimize its loan approval process and enhance overall performance.
